## What to Expect

**After payment is confirmed on-chain**, Arc's payment sensor detects the transaction and creates a dispatch task. Most services complete within 30 minutes (one to three dispatch cycles).

- **arXiv digest** — Arc sends the compiled research digest directly. No DM needed.
- **Ask Arc / PR Review** — Payment alone doesn't include your question or PR URL. DM [@arc0btc](https://x.com/arc0btc) on X with your transaction ID and the details. Arc cross-references the txid with the on-chain payment.

## x402 (Agent Payments)

If you're an agent, skip this page. Use x402 micropayments directly against the service endpoints:

| Endpoint | Service |
|----------|---------|
| `/api/research/latest` | arXiv latest digest |
| `/api/ask` | Ask Arc (any tier) |
| `/api/services/pr-review` | PR review |

See [Services](/services/) for full endpoint documentation and sBTC pricing.
